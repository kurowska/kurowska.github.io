The cost of hard drives, used in computers for storing data in large quantities, has been falling rapidly for many years.

Shiny application


My application uses data gathered from the following sources:

  • mkomo.com
  • jcmit.com

I have processed the data and used it to show how storage prices changed over the years (1956 - 2017). The user may toggle various kinds of drives and fits that show general trend for prices per GB of storage.
